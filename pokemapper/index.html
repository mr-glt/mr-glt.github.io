What Does PokéMapper Do?

This app is a live map that finds all the Pokemons around you. In real-time, it shows the locations of gyms, pokestops and Pokemons. The app has support for multiple accounts so you can speedup the scanning process and find more pokemon quicker. Your are able to filter by Pokemon, color of gym, defense of gym and more. It also shows lured pokestops. This app uses real-time data to give you the most accurate data no matter where you are. It is not dependent on others being near you. It is a great way to find rare pokemon or plan out routes! All of this is presented in a beautiful material package, for quick easy use.
